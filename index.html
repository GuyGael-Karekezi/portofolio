About Me

My name is Karekezi Guy Gael. I studied Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering at the University of Rwanda. During my studies, I worked on projects that helped me understand how systems work in real life, such as building a smart energy meter that lets households track electricity use in real time.

I am now studying for a Master of Science in Engineering Artificial Intelligence at Carnegie Mellon University Africa. My work focuses on machine learning, data analysis and building systems that use data to solve problems. I enjoy working with clean code, simple ideas and models that bring clear results. I like teamwork and learning from others because it helps me grow and build better solutions.

My goal is to create intelligent and practical tools that make life easier. I am interested in data driven systems, machine learning, IoT and projects that have a real impact. I always enjoy new ideas and exploring better ways to understand and use data.
